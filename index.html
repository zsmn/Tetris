Instructions

1) Use w to rotate each tetromino.

2) Use a to move each tetromino to the left.

3) Use d to move each tetromino to the right.

4) Use s to accelerate each tetromino to the bottom of the grid.

5) After filling up one entirely line, you earn 10 points.

6) Mode when you push NewGame: 20x20

7) Have Fun!
